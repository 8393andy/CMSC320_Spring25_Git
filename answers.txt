Q3: What is the difference between Merging and Rebasing?

Answer: Merge connects the separate branch to the main branch with a new
	commit, whereas rebase replays commits from a branch onto the main 
	branch, resulting in a single branch. Merge maintains more of the
	change history while rebase results in a cleaner history.
